Discovery FeedSwipe-friendly cards with instant YES/NO actions

Design 1: Discovery Feed

The primary screen users see on launch. Combines story-mode visual richness with WhatsApp-level speed. One-tap prediction directly from the feed — no drilling into a detail page required.

Fast Discovery Category pills at top let users filter instantly. Trending, Sports, Entertainment, Politics — one tap to switch context. Horizontal scroll works on $100 Android phones.
Instant Action Green YES / Red NO buttons are the largest tap targets on every card. Designed for thumb-zone reachability in one-handed use. No confirmation modal — tap and done, WhatsApp speed.
Social Proof Avatar stack shows which Tribe members already predicted. "Adebayo + 3 from your Tribe predicted" creates social pressure and FOMO that drives engagement.
Live Indicators Pulsing red "LIVE" badge on time-sensitive markets. Odds bar visualizes community sentiment at a glance — no numbers needed for low-literacy users.
Gen Z Optimized Dark theme, bold gradients, rounded cards, generous spacing. Visual language borrowed from Instagram and TikTok — familiar to 18-24 year olds. Minimal text, maximum visual.

Chat InteractionWhatsApp-speed discussion + inline predictions

Design 2: Chat-Style Predictions

Each prediction market doubles as a live chat room. The hybrid combines the speed and familiarity of WhatsApp with real-time odds and inline micro-predictions. Users can discuss, predict, and react — all in one flow.

WhatsApp-Speed UX Conversation bubbles, typing indicators, real-time messages. Users who've never used a prediction app instantly understand this interface because they've used WhatsApp for years.
Inline Micro-Predictions The "Quick Prediction" card drops into the chat at contextual moments (halftime, major event). One-tap to predict without leaving the conversation. Resolves in minutes.
Live Odds Banner Persistent odds display at top shows real-time movement. Big numbers, bold colors — scannable at a glance. Volume indicator shows market depth.
Cross-Platform Bridge "I just joined from WhatsApp!" — users can enter from WhatsApp bot deep link and land directly in the in-app chat. Seamless transition between chat-native and app experience.
System Messages as Context Match events ("HALFTIME: Arsenal 1-0 Chelsea") and odds shifts appear as system messages. Turns the chat into a live commentary stream that provides prediction context naturally.

My PredictionsLive updates + portfolio tracking

Design 3: Prediction Portfolio

A glanceable dashboard showing all active predictions with real-time updates. Designed to answer "what's happening with my bets?" in under 2 seconds.

Real-Time Update Badges Orange "Odds moved" and "New analysis" flash badges appear when something changes. Users see at a glance which predictions need attention — no need to open each one.
Stats at a Glance Three key metrics up top: accuracy percentage (motivates improvement), active count (engagement), and Tribe rank (social competition). All readable in under 1 second.
Color-Coded Momentum Green up-arrows when your position is strengthening, red down-arrows when weakening. Users instantly know if their predictions are trending well without reading numbers.
Resolved Section Recent wins and losses shown with virtual currency earned/lost. Creates a feedback loop: see results, learn from mistakes, predict better next time.
Tap to Chat Every prediction item is tappable — opens the chat view (Design 2) for that market. Seamless transition from portfolio monitoring to active discussion.

Pulse Widget + NotificationsLock screen predictions + contextual alerts

Design 4: Pulse & Ambient UX

Predictions that come to the user instead of requiring them to open the app. Lock screen widget + smart notifications create a "prediction pulse" throughout the day.

Lock Screen Widget The most relevant prediction appears on the lock screen with full context and YES/NO buttons. One interaction to predict — no unlocking, no app opening. Ultra-low data usage.
Contextual Timing Halftime notification arrives exactly when it's relevant. This is the "Pulse" — predictions that find you at the right moment based on live events, time, and location.
Update Notifications Three notification types: Resolved (green check — your result), Updates (chart — odds moved), Social (people — Tribe activity). Color-coded icons for instant scanning.
Data-Light Design The entire widget + notification stack uses under 5KB of data. Critical for Nigeria/Kenya where mobile data is pay-per-MB. Works with cached data when offline.
